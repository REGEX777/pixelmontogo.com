What is the

Pixelmon mod?

pixelmon is a open world multiplayer Pokémon game combined with the features of Minecraft. You can build your house and also catch all 800+ Pokémon's. In this Minecraft mod you can play super competitive because all moves + mega forms are in it.

With the pixelmon mod you can create your own goals if that is to build a beautiful home or to capture all the Pokémon's the choose is really yours.
